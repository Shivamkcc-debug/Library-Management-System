Welcome to the Library!

Here you can manage books and members.

Library Rules

  • Borrowing Limit: Max 3 books; return within 15 days.
  • Fines: ₹45 per day for overdue returns.
  • Membership: Only registered members can borrow books.
  • Silence: Maintain silence; phones on silent mode.
  • Damaged Books: Members are responsible for lost/damaged books.
  • Timings: Open 9:00 AM - 8:00 PM, Monday to Saturday.

Features

  • Book Management: Add and view books easily.
  • Member Management: Register and view members.
  • Book Issue/Return: Streamlined process with due date and fine tracking.
  • Dashboard: View issued books and member details at a glance.
